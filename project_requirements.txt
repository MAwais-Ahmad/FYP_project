DETAILED SCENARIO EXAMPLE
SCENARIO 1: "The School Festival Budget Crisis"
Story: (AI-generated, unique each time)
You're helping organize your school's annual festival. 
Budget: Rs. 50,000
5 club presidents want funding:
- Drama Club: Rs. 20,000 (stage props)
- Science Club: Rs. 18,000 (experiment materials)
- Sports Club: Rs. 15,000 (equipment)
- Art Club: Rs. 12,000 (supplies)
- Music Club: Rs. 10,000 (instruments rental)

Total requests = Rs. 75,000 (Rs. 25,000 over budget!)
Principal needs your recommendation by tomorrow.
PHASE 1: Understanding (2 questions)
Q1: [Timed - 90 seconds]
What is the main problem you need to solve?
[Open text box]

EXTRACT:
- time_to_start_typing
- response_length
- identified_core_issue (yes/no)
- mentioned_budget_constraint (yes/no)

---

Q2: [Timed - 60 seconds]
Which information is MOST critical for making a decision?
A) How much each club spent last year
B) Which clubs have the most members
C) What activities bring most student engagement
D) Which club presidents asked first

EXTRACT:
- decision_time
- choice_logic (data-driven vs emotional)
- answer_changed (yes/no)
PHASE 2: Planning (2 questions)
Q3: [Timed - 2 minutes]
List 3 possible solutions to this problem:
[3 text boxes]

EXTRACT:
- creativity_score (unique vs obvious solutions)
- practicality_score
- time_distribution (equal on all 3 or rushed last one?)
- solution_types (cut equally, prioritize, fundraise, etc.)

---

Q4: [Timed - 90 seconds]
Rank your solutions from best to worst and explain why:
[Drag-and-drop + short explanation]

EXTRACT:
- consistency_with_previous_answer
- reasoning_depth
- changes_made_during_ranking
PHASE 3: Execution (2 questions)
Q5: [Timed - 45 seconds - PRESSURE!]
URGENT: Drama Club president just called.

A) Accept immediately - more budget for others

EXTRACT:
- reaction_speed

- systematic_thinking (vs impulsive)
